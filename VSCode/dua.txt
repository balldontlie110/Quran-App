
اللَّهُمَّ انْفَعْنِي بِمَا عَلَّمْتَنِي وَعَلِّمْنِي مَا يَنْفَعُنِي
ALLAHUMMA INFA'NII BIMAA 'ALLAMTANII WA'ALLIMNII MAA YANFA'UUNII
Oh Allah! Make useful for me what You have taught me and teach me knowledge that will be useful to me.

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ فَهْمَ النَّبِيِّينَ وَحِفْظَ الْمُرْسَلِينَ وَالْمُقَرَّبِينَ
ALLAHUMMA INII AS'ALUKA FAHMAL-NABIYYEN WA HIFTHAL MURSALEEN AL-MUQARRABEEN
Oh Allah! I ask You for the understanding of the prophets and the memory of the messengers, and those nearest to You.

اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْ لِسَانِي عَامِرًا بِذِكْرِكَ وَقَلْبِي بِخَشْيَتِكَ
ALLAHUMMA IJ'AL LISANEE 'AMIRAN BI DHIKRIKA WA QALBI BI KHASHYATIKA
Oh Allah! Make my tongue full of Your remembrance and my heart with consciousness of You.

إِنَّكَ عَلَىٰ مَا تَشَاءُ قَدِيرٌ وَأَنْتَ حَسْبُنَا اللَّهُ وَنِعْمَ الْوَكِيلُ
INNAKA 'ALA MA-TASHA'U QADEER WA ANTA HASBUNA-ALLAHU WA NI'MAL WAKEEL
Oh Allah! You do whatever You wish, and You are my Availer and Protector and the best of aid.

اللَّهُمَّ لَا سَهْلَ إِلَّا مَا جَعَلْتَهُ سَهْلًا
ALLAHUMMA LA SAHLA ILLAMA JA-‘ALTAHU SAHLA
Oh Allah! Nothing is easy except what You have made easy.

تَجْعَلُ الْحَزَنَ إِذَا شِئْتَ سَهْلًا
TAJ‘ALU AL HAZANA ETHA MA SHI’TA SAHLA
If You wish, You can make the difficult easy.

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْتَوْدِعُكَ مَا قَرَأْتُ وَمَا حَفِظْتُ
ALLAHUMMA INNI ASTAODI'UKA MA QARA'TU WAMA HAFATH-TU
Oh Allah! I entrust You with what I have read and I have studied.

فَرُدَّهُ إِلَيَّ عِنْدَ حَاجَتِي إِلَيْهِ
FARUDDAHU 'ALLAYA INDA HAJATI ILAYHI
Oh Allah! Bring it back to me when I am in need of it.

إِنَّكَ عَلَىٰ مَا تَشَاءُ قَدِيرٌ وَأَنْتَ حَسْبُنَا اللَّهُ وَنِعْمَ الْوَكِيلُ
INNAKA 'ALA MA-TASHA'-U QADEER WA ANTA HASBUNA ALLAH WA NI'MAL WAKEEL
Oh Allah! You do whatever You wish, You are my Availer and Protector and the best of aid.
